# AD ICONIQ Mobile App - Interaction Design

## Core User Experience

### Primary Interactions

1. **Product Collection Browser**
   - Interactive product grid with category filters (Men's, Women's, Accessories, Sportswear)
   - Touch-friendly product cards with quick view and add to cart
   - Smooth horizontal scrolling through featured collections
   - Filter by price, color, size, and collection type

2. **Product Quick View Modal**
   - Tap any product to open detailed view with image gallery
   - Size selector with availability indicator
   - Color variant switching
   - Add to cart with quantity selector
   - Related products suggestions

3. **Shopping Cart & Checkout**
   - Slide-up cart panel with product thumbnails
   - Quantity adjustment and item removal
   - Running total calculation
   - Checkout form with customer information
   - Order confirmation system

4. **Collection Showcase Carousel**
   - Auto-scrolling hero carousel featuring seasonal collections
   - Manual swipe navigation
   - Collection-based navigation (La Flor Azul, Vantage, Essential)
   - Tap to explore full collection

## Secondary Interactions

5. **Newsletter Signup**
   - Prominent signup form with 15% discount incentive
   - Email validation and submission feedback
   - Privacy policy and terms integration

6. **Search & Discovery**
   - Search bar with auto-suggestions
   - Recent searches and popular items
   - Category-based browsing

7. **Customer Support Chat**
   - Floating chat button for customer service
   - Quick response templates for common questions

## User Journey Flow

1. **Landing** → Hero showcase with featured collections
2. **Browse** → Product discovery through categories and filters
3. **Select** → Product details and customization options
4. **Purchase** → Cart management and checkout process
5. **Engage** → Newsletter signup and customer support

## Mobile-First Considerations

- Touch-optimized button sizes (minimum 44px)
- Swipe gestures for product browsing
- Pull-to-refresh functionality
- Optimized image loading for mobile bandwidth
- Bottom navigation for easy thumb access
- Quick add-to-cart from product grid
- One-thumb navigation design
